The Haunting of Hollow Hill

On a dark and stormy night, a group of friends decided to explore the abandoned mansion on Hollow Hill. Legends spoke of a ghostly figure that roamed the halls, searching for something lost long ago...

As they entered the creaking doors, a chill ran down their spines. Shadows danced on the walls, and whispers echoed through the empty rooms. Little did they know, they were not alone...
